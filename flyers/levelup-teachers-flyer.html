The quickstart routine

  • 1 · Name the win: Call out the specific positive behavior in the moment (e.g., "Excellent focus during reading!").
  • 2 · Tap to award: Open your portal on your phone or desktop and add points in one tap, or scan a student barcode.
  • 3 · Watch them level up: Students scan cards at hallway kiosks to see points pop and milestones unlock.
  • 4 · Celebrate together: Host weekly raffle spins or let students trade points for exciting store prizes.

For classroom climate

  • Daily focus: Give students a clear, measurable goal to work toward every week.
  • Positive transitions: Project house standings or top students during transition blocks to keep energy positive.
