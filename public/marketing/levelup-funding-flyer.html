🏛️ State Claims & Discretionary Options

  • Option 3: NYS Nonpublic School Safety Equipment (NPSE) Grant
    Pillar: Security, checkpoints, and student management technology.
    Justification: Entrance checkpoints and touch kiosks function as a robust student accountability and campus movement tracking system.
    Process: Purchase with general supply accounts, then submit hardware receipts for 100% reimbursement under safety claims.
  • Option 4: NYS Mandated Services Reimbursement (MSR)
    Pillar: Covering administrative costs of mandated pupil tracking.
    Justification: Automated arrival scans, barcode logging, and pupil reporting sheets map directly under Mandated Service #1: Pupil Attendance Report (PAR).
    Process: Subsidize ongoing software licensing against your annual MSR claims.
  • Option 5: Discretionary Tax Levy & Technology Fees
    Pillar: Open, frictionless purchasing with zero administrative hurdles.
    Process: Direct open-market PO approval by the principal utilizing general supplies or student tech fee reserves.
