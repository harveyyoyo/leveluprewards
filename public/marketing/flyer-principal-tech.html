Technology & safety

Built for schools that must prove compliance—not just promise it.

LevelUp Rewards runs on enterprise cloud infrastructure with school-scoped data isolation, documented privacy protocols, and controls your leadership team can review before sign-off.

Infrastructure

  • Google Cloud / Firebase (US-East multi-region)
  • School-scoped Firestore paths per tenant
  • Session cookies + role-based staff access
  • Cloudflare edge protection (sub-processor)

Encryption & retention

  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • TLS 1.3 in transit
  • Data purged within 90 days of contract end
  • 7-day breach notification commitment

Privacy controls

  • FERPA & COPPA-aligned data rubric (DPSA)
  • NY Education Law 2-d supplemental terms
  • Public display: full name or preferred-only mode
  • Optional parent weekly digest (opt-in per student)

AI safety (2026 standards)

  • Student PII never used to train LLMs
  • Zero-retention APIs for AI-assisted features
  • 1EdTech Generative AI Data Rubric aligned
  • Age-appropriate guardrails on student AI fun

Operational safeguards

  • Admin audit log for sensitive actions
  • Student portal lockout after failed passcodes
  • Teacher offline award queue with sync
  • CSV/JSON import—no ad-hoc spreadsheets

What we collect (purpose-limited)

  • Identity: name, grade—for ledger & kiosk
  • Behavioral: points, coupons, redemptions
  • Technical: login timestamps—for security
