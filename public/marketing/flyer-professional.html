Cloud-based PBIS rewards for modern schools

LevelUp EDU connects student kiosks, teacher stations, and admin tools in one secure platform. Award points in seconds, track coupon usage, and give students a motivating rewards experience backed by real-time data.

Platform capabilities

  • Student kiosk — NFC cards, barcode scans, and quick ID login with on-the-spot redemption.
  • Teacher station — Generate and print coupon codes; consistent PBIS across classrooms.
  • Admin portal — Manage roster, categories, imports/exports, and school-wide settings.
  • School office — Grades, billing, and shared roster tools for front-office staff.
  • Cloud sync — Firebase-backed data stays consistent across every device.
