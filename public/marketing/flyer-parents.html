LevelUp Rewards

For parents & families

Your child earns rewards for doing
the right thing at school.

LevelUp is a digital PBIS reward system your school uses to recognize positive behavior. When your child makes great choices, teachers award points they can redeem for real prizes—giving them something meaningful to work toward every day.

How it works

1

Teacher awards points for positive behavior

2

Points add up in your child's account

3

Student visits the kiosk to check balance

4

Redeems points for prizes in the shop

Is it safe?

Yes—students log in using their school ID, NFC card, or a barcode. No personal passwords shared publicly.

What kinds of prizes?

Prizes are set by your school—homework passes, privileges, stickers, small items, and more.

Can I see my child's points?

Ask your school admin about home portal access for families at your campus.

Who runs this?

LevelUp EdTech Enterprises LLC. Contact us at contact@leveluprewards.app.

Questions for your child's school? Ask the front office about LevelUp.
