Staff handout

LevelUp keeps positive reinforcement fast, visible, and consistent. Use this four-step rhythm to award points, build classroom momentum, and make rewards feel real for students.

1Name the behavior you want to see again.
2Award points in the moment or after the activity.
3Let students check balance, rank, and shop options.
4Celebrate redemptions, raffle wins, and streaks.

Keep it simple

  • Pick 2 or 3 focus behaviors for the week.
  • Award small amounts often instead of waiting for perfect days.
  • Use raffle moments for quick classroom energy.
  • Check reports with your PBIS lead when patterns appear.
Try saying

"I noticed you helped reset the table without being asked. That is responsibility."

Try asking

"Which reward are you saving for, and what is one behavior that gets you closer?"
